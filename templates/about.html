About Senyasalin

Our Mission

Senyasalin is built to make Filipino Sign Language learning accessible, practical, and engaging through reliable AI guidance.

Building Inclusive Technology

We are creating a learner-friendly platform where people can practice Filipino Sign Language with real-time visual feedback, clear guidance, and a consistent experience that supports everyday progress.

What We Deliver

  • Real-time recognition and corrective feedback
  • Instructional videos for guided sign practice
  • Progress tracking with summaries and streak insights
  • Account-based history to review improvement over time
